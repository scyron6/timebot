Welcome

Welcome to TimeBot! We pride ourselves on making a user friendly application. After this short tutorial, you will understand all our site has to offer! I am using a sample excel file that I crafted for this tutorial, but please follow along with your own Karbon export!


Empty Dashboard

When you first reach the dashboard, you will see the page that is displayed in the screenshot below. The main thing to notice are the two date fields in the upper right corner. These dates filter out the information that is shown in your tables. It will default to the current month, but you can change them by clicking the calendar next to the date.


Timesheets

Now click on Timesheets in the left-hand navigation. This will take you to a page similar to the screenshot below. This page displays all of your timesheets, the date range they contain, and the date the timesheet was entered. Press 'Add New' to input a new timesheet.


Input Timesheet

Clicking 'Add New' while on the Timesheets page will open a box to input a new timesheet. It will prompt you for the date range that the timesheet contains and an excel file. To note, the dates that you input here are solely for your organizational purposes. Each entry will have a date that is read from the excel sheet regardless of the dates you select here.


After you click 'Submit', you will see the timesheet be automatically added to the timesheets table. If you input a big excel file, this could take a moment. Clicking on any row in the Timesheets table will take you to a page with more detailed information. Please click on the timesheet you just submitted.


Timesheet Details

In the screenshot below, you will see all the entries from the timesheet you just submitted. These are sorted by date but not organized in any way; that's what the other tables are for! You can also delete all entries from the timesheet by clicking 'Delete Timesheet' in the upper right-hand corner. Be careful though, there is no way to retrieve these records once deleted. You will have to submit the timesheet again.


Clients

Navigate to the Clients table by clicking 'Clients' in the left-hand navigation. This will show you a page similar to the screenshot below. This table will show you the name of the client and total time spent on that client. Clicking on any client will bring you to a more detailed page. Please click on any client.


Client Details

The detailed view of a client is shown in the screenshot below. It displays every work entry for that client within the selected dates. For each entry, it displays the employee, work label, time, and date.


Wrap Up

You are now ready to use TimeBot. Detailed views are available for the Clients, Employees, Roles, Tasks, and Timesheets tables. If you click on any row in one those table, you will be brought to a more detailed view. For support or feedback, please email thetimebot@protonmail.com!
